Customise ...all that darknessy goodness.

Overview

BootstrapDark comes packed with five default colours that have been pre compiled for ease of use. Simply link to the bootsrap-{color}.css in your project, and you'll be away.

Custom colors

If you wish to use your own colors, you must modify the @linkColor in the variables.less file which can be found in the less directory of the download. - Finally, compile bootstrap.less with your favorite compiler. I use Crunch or SimpLESS.
